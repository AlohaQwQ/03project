How Does SLERF Dao Voting Work?

  1. Each SLERF NFT that you hold represents 1 vote within SLERF DAO proposals.
  2. Each NFT can only be used to vote once. This means if you transfer your NFT to another wallet after voting it cannot be used to vote again. This goes for sales as well.
  3. Votes cannot be changed.
